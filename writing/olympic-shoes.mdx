## Notes

1. Jesse Gruper used Skwamas for Boulder and Solution for Lead
2. Sasha Lehmann used Solutions for Boulder and TN Pro for Lead
3. Adam Ondra used Solutions Comps for Boulder and Lead except for B4 (semi-finals) where he used Ondra Pros 
4. Tomoa Narasaki used Flagship Pros for B1/B2/B3, and TN Pros for B4 (semi-finals) and Lead 
5. Chae-hyun Seo used Theorys for Boulder and Solution Comps for Lead
6. Brooke Raboutou used Skwamas for Boulder and Solutions for Lead
7. Miho Nonaka used a [customized Five Ten Aleon with a double strap](https://www.mountainproject.com/forum/topic/122473781/new-510-shoe-spotted-at-world-cup)
